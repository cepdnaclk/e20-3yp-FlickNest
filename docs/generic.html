About FlickNest

FlickNest redefines smart automation with gesture-based controls, offering an inclusive and hands-free solution for managing smart devices.

FlickNest is a gesture-controlled smart automation system designed to provide a seamless and intuitive way to interact with smart devices. Unlike traditional methods relying on mobile apps or voice commands, FlickNest leverages advanced gesture recognition technology to make automation more accessible, especially in noisy environments or for individuals with disabilities.

Our system consists of a smart bracelet equipped with sensors, an ESP32 microcontroller, and a mobile app that translates gestures into actionable commands. The mobile app utilizes machine learning to ensure high accuracy and responsiveness, while communication with devices is achieved via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi protocols.

With FlickNest, we aim to empower users by offering a secure, privacy-focused, and efficient solution for smart home automation.
